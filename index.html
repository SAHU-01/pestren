01 · The proof

Stop guessing. Get answers that cite their sources.

A general model reasons in the abstract. Pestren resolves the field, cites the sources, and returns an answer your agent can act on — for any crop, anywhere.

A plain LLM
Q: Is the maize at 0.52, 35.27 at risk of fall armyworm this week?
I don't have real-time access to weather or field data. Fall armyworm risk generally rises in warm, humid conditions… you may want to scout your fields. Would you like general prevention tips?
no source / no date / generic
PestrenLive
pestren.ask("fall armyworm risk for maize at 0.52, 35.27 this week?")
Elevated fall-armyworm pressure this week — warm nights + recent rain on a field at whorl stage.
risk = 74 / 100 (elevated) crop = maize (verified · whorl stage) driver = warm nights + rainfall field = parcel 1.2 ha (resolved polygon) source = FAO FAW-Monitor + national ag dept ↗ fetched = 2026-07-01 confidence = field-verified action = scout now; targeted bio-control if >5% damage
cited / timestamped / verified / actionable

Every answer ends in an action — and, when you want it, the exact product to buy and how to set it up.
